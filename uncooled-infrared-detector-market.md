<p><p>The uncooled infrared detector market can be divided into various types including bolometer, pyroelectric detector, thermopile detector, diode detector, thermal capacitance detector, and other detectors. Bolometers measure the heating effect of infrared radiation, pyroelectric detectors generate a voltage in response to temperature change, thermopile detectors use multiple thermocouples to detect infrared radiation, diode detectors convert infrared radiation into an electrical signal, and thermal capacitance detectors measure the change in heat capacity. Other detectors may include microbolometers, quantum well infrared photodetectors, and thermistor detectors.</p></p>
