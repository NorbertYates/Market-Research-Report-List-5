<p><p>LCoS Spatial Light Modulators (LCOS-SLM) are devices used in various applications such as holography, microscopy, and optical communication. There are two main types of LCOS-SLM: Reflective LCOS-SLM and Transmissive LCOS-SLM. Reflective LCOS-SLM reflects incident light off an array of micro mirrors, allowing precise control over the phase and intensity of the light. Transmissive LCOS-SLM, on the other hand, modulates light passing through liquid crystal cells. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on their design and capabilities.</p></p>
