<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grapples for excavators are available in two main types - hydraulic and mechanical. Hydraulic grapples are powered by the excavator's hydraulic system, offering more precision and control during operation. They are ideal for handling heavier loads and are often used in construction and demolition applications. On the other hand, mechanical grapples rely on the excavator's mechanical power for operation. They are simpler in design and more cost-effective, making them suitable for lighter tasks such as landscaping and yard maintenance.</p></p>
