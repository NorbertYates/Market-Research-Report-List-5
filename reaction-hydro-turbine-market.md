<p><p>Reaction Hydro Turbine market is segmented into four types based on their design and functionality. The Mixed Flow Hydro Turbine combines features of both axial and radial flow turbines for efficient energy conversion. Axial Flow Hydro Turbine has blades that rotate around a central axis, allowing water to flow parallel to the axis. Inclined Flow Hydro Turbine uses an inclined shaft for water flow, increasing energy efficiency. Tubular Hydro Turbine has a cylindrical casing that houses the turbine components, providing a compact and streamline design.</p></p>
