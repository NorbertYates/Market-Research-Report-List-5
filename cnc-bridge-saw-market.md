<p><p>CNC Bridge Saw market is categorized into three types based on the number of axes - 3 Axis, 4 Axis, and 5 Axis. In the 3 Axis market, the saw can move along X, Y, and Z axes for cutting and shaping tasks. The 4 Axis market includes the additional capability of rotating the cutting tool, allowing for more complex cuts. In the 5 Axis market, the saw can tilt and rotate the cutting tool, enabling even more intricate and precise cutting operations. These variations cater to different levels of cutting precision and complexity.</p></p>
