<p><p>Dairy enzymes market consists of various types such as lactase, chymosin, microbial rennet, and lipase. Lactase is used to break down lactose in dairy products for lactose-intolerant individuals. Chymosin is commonly used in cheese production to coagulate milk. Microbial rennet is an alternative to traditional animal rennet for cheese production. Lipase is used to enhance flavor in cheese and dairy products. Each type of enzyme serves a specific purpose in the dairy industry, contributing to the quality and variety of dairy products available.</p></p>
