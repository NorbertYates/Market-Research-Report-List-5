<p><p>Baghouses market consists of three main types of bag filters: Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter, Reverse Air Bag Filter, and Pulse Jet Bag Filter. Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter uses a mechanical arm to shake the bags and remove the dust. Reverse Air Bag Filter uses reverse airflow to clean the bags. Pulse Jet Bag Filter uses bursts of compressed air to clean the bags. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on the level of efficiency and ease of maintenance required.</p></p>
