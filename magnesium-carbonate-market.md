<p><p>Magnesium Carbonate market is divided into two main types: Light Magnesium Carbonate and Heavy Magnesium Carbonate. Light Magnesium Carbonate, also known as magnesium hydroxide carbonate, is used in cosmetics, medicine, and food additives due to its low density and high reactivity. On the other hand, Heavy Magnesium Carbonate, also known as magnesite, is utilized in refractory materials and fertilizers due to its high density and heat resistance properties. Both types play a vital role in various industries based on their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
