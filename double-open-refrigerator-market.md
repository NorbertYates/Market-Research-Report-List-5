<p><ul><li>Direct-cooled</li><li>Air-cooled</li><li>Mixed Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double-open refrigerator market can be categorized into three main types based on their cooling mechanisms: direct-cooled, air-cooled, and mixed refrigeration. Direct-cooled refrigerators utilize cooling coils directly on the shelves, providing uniform temperature throughout the interior. Air-cooled refrigerators, on the other hand, circulate cold air to maintain consistent temperatures. Mixed refrigeration combines both direct cooling and air circulation for optimal temperature control. Each type offers distinct advantages and is suited for different storage needs and preferences.</p></p>
