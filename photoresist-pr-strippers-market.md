<p><strong>The Photoresist (PR) Strippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoresist (PR) Strippers are chemicals used in semiconductor manufacturing to remove photoresist material from wafers after photolithography processes. Positive PR Strippers are used to dissolve and remove positive photoresist materials from wafers, while Negative PR Strippers are used for negative photoresist materials. Positive PR Strippers are typically alkaline-based, while Negative PR Strippers are acidic-based. Both types of PR Strippers play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process by ensuring proper cleaning and preparation of wafers for subsequent fabrication steps.</p></p>
