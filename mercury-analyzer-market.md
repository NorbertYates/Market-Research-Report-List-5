<p><p>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer are two types of mercury analyzers used in various industries for measuring trace levels of mercury in different samples. While the former utilizes atomic absorption spectroscopy to detect mercury vapor, the latter uses atomic fluorescence spectroscopy to measure elemental mercury. Both analyzers are widely used in environmental monitoring, food safety testing, and research applications due to their high sensitivity, accuracy, and reliability in detecting mercury contamination.</p></p>
