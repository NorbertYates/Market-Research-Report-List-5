<p><ul><li>Hot-end Coatings</li><li>Cold-end Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass coatings for containers are divided into two main types: hot-end coatings and cold-end coatings. Hot-end coatings are applied during the glass forming process to protect the glass from damage caused by high temperatures. Cold-end coatings, on the other hand, are applied after the glass has been formed to provide additional protection against environmental factors such as scratching and corrosion. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in extending the lifespan of glass containers and maintaining their quality.</p></p>
