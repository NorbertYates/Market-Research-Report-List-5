<p><p>Safety capacitors are classified into two main types: Class-X Capacitors and Class-Y Capacitors. Class-X capacitors are designed for use in applications where failure could result in electric shock, such as in power supplies. Class-Y capacitors are used in applications where failure could lead to the creation of electrical noise or interference. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and proper functioning of electronic devices by filtering out unwanted signals and providing insulation against electrical shocks.</p></p>
