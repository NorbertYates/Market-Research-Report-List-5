<p><p>The IoT Network Module market consists of various types such as Lora, NB-IoT, LTE-M, Wi-Fi, and others. Lora is a long-range low-power wireless platform ideal for IoT applications. NB-IoT is designed for low-power wide-area networks. LTE-M enables low-power IoT devices to connect to cellular networks. Wi-Fi provides high-speed connectivity for IoT devices. Other types in this market include Zigbee, Bluetooth, and Sigfox, each offering unique features and applications for IoT devices.</p></p>
