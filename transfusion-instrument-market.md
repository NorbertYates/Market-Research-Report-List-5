<p><ul><li>Volumetric Infusion Pump</li><li>Gravity-delivered Infusion Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transfusion instrument market consists of two main types: volumetric infusion pumps and gravity-delivered infusion pumps. Volumetric infusion pumps accurately control the rate and volume of fluids being administered to patients through an electronic system, providing precision and consistency in drug delivery. On the other hand, gravity-delivered infusion pumps rely on gravity to passively infuse fluids into the patient, offering a simpler and cost-effective alternative. Both types play crucial roles in medical settings, catering to different needs based on the situation and requirements.</p></p>
