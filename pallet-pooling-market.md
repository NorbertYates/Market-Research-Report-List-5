<p><ul><li>Tray static leasing</li><li>Tray dynamic flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pallet pooling market types can be categorized into tray static leasing and tray dynamic flow models. Tray static leasing involves the fixed rental of pallets for a specific period of time, providing stability and predictability for businesses. On the other hand, tray dynamic flow markets allow for the flexible exchange of pallets based on demand, enabling more efficient and cost-effective pallet management. Both models offer benefits for businesses looking to streamline their supply chain operations and optimize their pallet usage.</p></p>
