<p><p>Hydraulic brake hoses are available in two main types – Nylon Core and PTEE Core. Nylon Core hoses are known for their durability, flexibility, and resistance to expansion under pressure. They are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. On the other hand, PTEE Core hoses are known for their exceptional chemical resistance, high temperature tolerance, and low friction properties. They are often used in high-performance racing cars and aircraft applications. Both types offer reliable brake performance and safety, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
