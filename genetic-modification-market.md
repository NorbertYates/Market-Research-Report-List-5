<p><p>Genetic Modification Market Types include various technologies such as Molecular Cloning, Gene Delivery, and Genome Editing. Molecular Cloning involves replicating DNA fragments and inserting them into host organisms. Gene Delivery focuses on introducing foreign genes into a host organism through various methods like viral vectors or gene guns. Genome Editing allows for precise modification of an organism's genetic material, typically using tools like CRISPR-Cas9. These technologies play crucial roles in research, agriculture, and medicine by allowing scientists to manipulate and study genetic information.</p></p>
