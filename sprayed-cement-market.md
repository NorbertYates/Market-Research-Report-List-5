<p><strong>The Sprayed Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Spraying</li><li>Wet Spraying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sprayed cement market includes both dry spraying and wet spraying methods. Dry spraying involves mixing dry cement with water at the nozzle before spraying onto the surface, while wet spraying involves pre-mixing wet cement before spraying. Dry spraying is preferred for overhead and vertical applications due to its faster setting time, while wet spraying is more commonly used for large projects such as tunnel linings and slope stabilization. Both methods offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for various construction and repair projects.</p></p>
