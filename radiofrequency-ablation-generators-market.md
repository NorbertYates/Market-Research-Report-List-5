<p><p>Radiofrequency ablation generators come in two main types: monopolar and multi-polar fashion. Monopolar generators use a single electrode to deliver radiofrequency energy to targeted areas for tissue ablation. On the other hand, multi-polar generators utilize multiple electrodes to provide a more uniform and controlled energy distribution, enabling more precise and effective treatment of larger or irregularly shaped tissues. Both types of generators are essential tools in minimally invasive procedures for treating various medical conditions such as tumors and chronic pain.</p></p>
