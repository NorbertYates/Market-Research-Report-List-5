<p><strong>The Polished Concrete Floor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Polished Concrete Floor</li><li>Exposed Aggregate Polished Concrete Floor</li><li>Fully Exposed Aggregate Polished Concrete Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polished Concrete Floor market can be categorized into three main types:</p><p>1. Top Polished Concrete Floor: This type is achieved by grinding down the concrete surface and then polishing it to a smooth finish. It is a cost-effective option for a sleek and durable floor.</p><p>2. Exposed Aggregate Polished Concrete Floor: This type involves removing the top layer of concrete to reveal the aggregate underneath, resulting in a decorative and textured finish.</p><p>3. Fully Exposed Aggregate Polished Concrete Floor: This type exposes the entire aggregate surface for a more dramatic and visually appealing look. It is a popular choice for high-end residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
