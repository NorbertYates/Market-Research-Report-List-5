<p><ul><li>Embolic Coils</li><li>Liquid Agents</li><li>EPDs</li><li>Flow Diverters</li><li>Access Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embolization devices market includes various types such as Embolic Coils, Liquid Agents, Embolic Protection Devices (EPDs), Flow Diverters, and Access Devices. Embolic coils are used to block blood flow in aneurysms or blood vessels. Liquid agents like embolic liquids or glue are used to block blood vessels. EPDs are used to protect against embolization during procedures. Flow diverters are used to redirect blood flow away from an aneurysm. Access devices are used to gain access to blood vessels for procedures.</p></p>
