<p><p>Spectral filters are used in various industries for manipulating light for different purposes. Dichroic filters are designed to reflect certain wavelengths while transmitting others. Neutral Density (ND) filters reduce the intensity of light without affecting its color. Band-pass filters only allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass through. Other market segments for spectral filters include long-pass filters, short-pass filters, and edge filters, each used for specific applications such as fluorescence microscopy, astronomy, or machine vision. These filters play a crucial role in controlling and enhancing the quality of light in various devices and equipment.</p></p>
