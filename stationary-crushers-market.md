<p><p>Stationary crushers are commonly classified into two types, hydraulic crushers and pneumatic crushers. Hydraulic crushers use a hydraulic system to apply pressure to crush materials, while pneumatic crushers use air pressure. Both types are typically used for heavy-duty industrial applications where continuous and efficient crushing is required. Hydraulic crushers are known for their high crushing force and precision, while pneumatic crushers are valued for their simplicity and ease of maintenance. Both types offer reliable crushing capabilities for various materials.</p></p>
