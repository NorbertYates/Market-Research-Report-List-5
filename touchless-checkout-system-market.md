<p><p>A Touchless Checkout System is a technology that allows customers to scan and pay for their purchases without the need for human interaction. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for convenient and contactless shopping experiences, especially in light of the COVID-19 pandemic. Consumers are increasingly looking for ways to minimize physical contact in public spaces, and touchless checkout systems offer a safe and efficient solution.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Touchless Checkout System Market include the integration of mobile payment options, the use of smart shelves and RFID technology to track inventory and customer purchases, and the development of autonomous checkout solutions that do not require any manual scanning or payment processing.
