<p><p>Advanced Phase Change Materials (PCM) can be classified into three main types: Organic, Inorganic, and Bio-based. Organic PCMs are derived from carbon-based compounds such as paraffins and fatty acids, offering high thermal storage capacity. Inorganic PCMs, on the other hand, are typically salts or metallic compounds with excellent thermal conductivity. Bio-based PCMs are made from renewable resources such as plant oils and sugars, making them environmentally friendly options for thermal energy storage applications. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for various industries.</p></p>
