<p><p>Drum cutters are commonly used attachments for excavators in various industries such as construction and mining. There are two main types of drum cutters available in the market: transverse and axial. Transverse drum cutters are designed to cut perpendicular to the axis of the excavator arm, providing efficient cutting in tight spaces. On the other hand, axial drum cutters are designed to cut parallel to the arm axis, allowing for precision cutting and shaping of surfaces. Both types of drum cutters offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of the job.</p></p>
