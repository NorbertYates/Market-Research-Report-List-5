Refrigerated storage facilities maintain slightly lower temperatures to keep items like meats and seafood fresh for longer periods. Freezer markets store goods at much lower temperatures, typically below 0 degrees Fahrenheit, to preserve items like frozen meals, ice cream, and other frozen products. Each type of cold storage facility plays a crucial role in maintaining the quality and safety of perishable goods.</p></p>
