<p><p>A V-Belt Clutch is a mechanical device used to transfer power between two shafts in a machine. It consists of a V-shaped belt that is wrapped around a pair of pulleys, with one pulley connected to the input shaft and the other connected to the output shaft. When the input shaft rotates, it causes the belt to move, which in turn rotates the output shaft.</p><p>The V-Belt Clutch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. <p><p>The V-Belt Clutch Market is segmented into two main types: Dry Type and Wet Type. Dry Type V-Belt Clutches utilize friction to transfer power without the use of any lubricant, making them low maintenance and cost-effective. Wet Type V-Belt Clutches, on the other hand, use a lubricant to reduce heat and wear, providing smoother engagement and longer lifespan. Both types offer unique advantages based on application requirements, with Dry Type being suitable for simpler systems and Wet Type offering better performance in high-demand environments.</p></p>
