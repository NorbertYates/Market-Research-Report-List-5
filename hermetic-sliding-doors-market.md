<p><ul><li>Single Leaf</li><li>Double Leaf</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors come in two main types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel that slides to the side to open and close. These are commonly used in smaller spaces where space-saving is a priority. Double leaf doors, on the other hand, consist of two panels that slide to the side, creating a larger opening than single leaf doors. This type is often used in larger spaces where a wider entrance is needed. Both types provide airtight seals, making them ideal for environments where hygiene and air quality are crucial.</p></p>
