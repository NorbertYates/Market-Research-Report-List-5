<p><p>PTCA guide wires are used in percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty procedures to navigate through blood vessels. There are two main types of guide wires: solid steel wires and nitinol core wires. Solid steel wires are stiff and provide good torque control, while nitinol core wires have a greater flexibility due to their shape memory properties. Both types of guide wires are essential tools in interventional cardiology procedures, allowing for precise navigation and positioning within the arteries.</p></p>
