<p><p>Masticating juicers are available in two main types: Vertical Auger Models and Horizontal Auger Models. Vertical Auger Models are designed with a vertical auger that extracts juice by crushing and squeezing fruits and vegetables. This type of juicer is known for its efficiency in extracting juice and producing less foam. Horizontal Auger Models, on the other hand, have a horizontal auger that functions similarly to the vertical model but may have a different feeding system. Both types offer different features and benefits to suit the specific needs of consumers.</p></p>
