<p><p>Specialty PACS Market Types include On-Premise and Cloud solutions. On-Premise PACS systems store data on local servers within the healthcare facility, providing complete control over data security but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud PACS solutions store data on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. Cloud PACS also provide accessibility from any location with internet connectivity, making it easier to collaborate and share medical images across different institutions.</p></p>
