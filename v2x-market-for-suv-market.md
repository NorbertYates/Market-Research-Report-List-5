<p><p>The V2X market for SUVs includes three main types: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V), Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I), and Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P). V2V technology allows SUVs to communicate with other vehicles on the road to improve safety and traffic flow. V2I enables SUVs to connect with infrastructure such as traffic lights and road signs for better navigation. V2P involves SUVs communicating with pedestrians, alerting them of the vehicle's presence for enhanced pedestrian safety. These technologies work together to create a more connected and safer driving experience for SUVs.</p></p>
