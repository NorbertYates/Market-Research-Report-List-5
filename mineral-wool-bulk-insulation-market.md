<p><p>Mineral Wool Bulk Insulation comes in two main types: Light Density Mineral Wool and Heavy Density Mineral Wool. Light Density Mineral Wool has lower thermal conductivity and is more flexible, making it easier to install in tight spaces. On the other hand, Heavy Density Mineral Wool has higher thermal resistance and is more rigid, providing better soundproofing and fire resistance. Both types offer excellent thermal insulation properties, but the choice between them depends on specific project requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
