<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaic Skylights come in two main types: Crystalline Panel and Thin Film Panel. Crystalline Panel skylights use silicon wafers to convert sunlight into electricity and are known for their high efficiency and durability. Thin Film Panel skylights, on the other hand, use a thin layer of semiconductor material to generate electricity and are often more flexible and lightweight. Both types of skylights provide a sustainable solution for generating electricity while allowing natural light to enter the building.</p></p>
