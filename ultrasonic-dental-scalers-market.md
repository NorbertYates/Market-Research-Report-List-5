<p><p>Ultrasonic dental scalers are used in dental cleanings to remove plaque and tartar from teeth. There are two main types of ultrasonic scalers on the market: Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers and Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers. Magnetostrictive scalers use a magnetic field to vibrate a metal stack, creating ultrasonic vibrations. Piezoelectric scalers use crystals that expand and contract in response to an electric current, producing ultrasonic vibrations. Both types of scalers are effective in removing stubborn plaque and tartar for improved dental hygiene.</p></p>
