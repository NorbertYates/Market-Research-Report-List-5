<p><p>Automotive Vacuum Brake Boosters are essential components that assist in boosting the braking power of vehicles by amplifying the force applied to the brake pedal. There are three main types of vacuum brake boosters: Camshaft-driven, Engine-driven, and Electric. Camshaft-driven boosters use the engine's camshaft to generate vacuum, Engine-driven boosters utilize the engine's vacuum pump, and Electric boosters employ an electric pump to create vacuum. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of performance, efficiency, and compatibility with different vehicle models.</p></p>
