<p><p>Knuckleboom cranes are classified into three main types based on boom length: small boom cranes, medium boom cranes, and long boom cranes. Small boom cranes are compact and versatile, suitable for confined spaces and light lifting tasks. Medium boom cranes have a balance of reach and power, ideal for medium-sized construction projects. Long boom cranes are designed for heavy-duty lifting and long reach capabilities, commonly used in industries such as shipbuilding and oil and gas. Each type caters to specific lifting requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
