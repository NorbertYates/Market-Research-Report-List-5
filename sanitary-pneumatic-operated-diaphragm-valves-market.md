<p><p>Sanitary pneumatic operated diaphragm valves come in three main types based on their connection methods: threaded connection, welded connection, and flanged connection. Threaded connection valves are easy to install and remove, making them ideal for applications where frequent maintenance is needed. Welded connection valves provide a secure, leak-proof seal and are commonly used in high-pressure systems. Flanged connection valves are versatile and can easily be connected to various piping systems, offering a high level of customization and flexibility.</p></p>
