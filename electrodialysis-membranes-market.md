<p><p>Electrodialysis membranes are used in various industrial processes for separating ions from different solutions. Heterogeneous membranes consist of multiple layers with different properties, providing selective ion transport. Homogeneous membranes have a uniform structure for ion separation. Bipolar membranes have both anion and cation exchange layers, allowing for the production of acid and base streams simultaneously.
