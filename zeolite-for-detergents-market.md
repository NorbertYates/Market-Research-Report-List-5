<p><p>Zeolite for detergents market includes various types such as Zeolite A, Zeolite P, Zeolite X, and Zeolite AX. Zeolite A is widely used in powdered laundry detergents for its high calcium ion exchange capacity. Zeolite P is preferred for its low phosphate content and eco-friendliness. Zeolite X is known for its high detergency and water softening properties. Zeolite AX is a combination of Zeolite A and Zeolite X, offering both calcium ion exchange capacity and detergency benefits.</p></p>
