<p><p>Total Hydrocarbon Analyzers Market Types include Flame Ionization Detector (FID), Gas Chromatographs (GCs), Catalytic Sensors, Photo-Ionization (PID) and Infra-Red (IR) Sensors. FID measures total hydrocarbons by burning a sample in a flame and measuring the resulting ions. GCs separate and quantify individual hydrocarbons in a sample. Catalytic sensors detect hydrocarbons based on their ability to catalyze a chemical reaction. PID sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize and detect hydrocarbons. IR sensors detect hydrocarbons based on their absorption of infrared light.</p></p>
