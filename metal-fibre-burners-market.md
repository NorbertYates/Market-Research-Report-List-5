<p><p>Metal fibre burners are available in two main types - Infrared mode type and Blue flame mode type. Infrared mode type burners emit infrared radiation for heating, which is absorbed by objects and surfaces, providing focused and efficient heat. On the other hand, Blue flame mode type burners use a traditional blue flame to heat the surrounding air, providing more widespread heat. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific heating requirements.</p></p>
