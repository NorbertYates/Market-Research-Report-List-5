<p><p>Fire resistant fluids are classified into different types based on their composition and properties. High water fluids are primarily made up of water and offer good fire resistance. Water glycol fluids contain a mixture of water and glycol, providing a balance between fire resistance and lubricating properties. Synthetic fire resistant fluids are chemically engineered to provide optimal fire protection. Other types of fire resistant fluids in the market include phosphate ester-based fluids, silicone-based fluids, and vegetable oil-based fluids, each offering specific advantages for different applications.</p></p>
