<p><p>ANC headphones come in three market types based on their design: In Ear, On Ear, and Over Ear. In Ear headphones are small, lightweight, and fit directly into the ear canal, providing portability and decent noise isolation. On Ear headphones sit on the outer ear, offering a balance between portability and comfort while still providing good sound quality. Over Ear headphones cover the entire ear, delivering superior noise cancellation and sound quality, making them ideal for long listening sessions.</p></p>
