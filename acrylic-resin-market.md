<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic resin market can be divided into two main types: thermosetting acrylic resin and thermoplastic acrylic resin. Thermosetting acrylic resin is a type of resin that cures irreversibly when heated, forming a durable and rigid material. On the other hand, thermoplastic acrylic resin softens when heated and hardens again when cooled, allowing for reshaping and recycling. Both types of acrylic resins have specific applications in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
