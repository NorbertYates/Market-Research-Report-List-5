<p><p>Manual welders are commonly used in projection welding and spot welding markets. In projection welding, the welder uses electrodes to concentrate the heat on a specific area, allowing for precise and strong welds. In spot welding, the welder uses two electrodes to apply pressure and heat to two pieces of metal, creating a strong bond. Both types of welding are manual processes that require skill and precision to achieve quality welds. The projection welding and spot welding markets continue to be popular choices for manual welders in various industries.</p></p>
