<p><p>Optical Fiber Identifiers market is divided into two main types: OFI-BIPM and OFI-BI. OFI-BIPM identifies optical fibers based on their presence or absence of traffic signals, making it suitable for both live and dark fibers. On the other hand, OFI-BI identifies fibers based on their presence of traffic signals, making it ideal for live fibers. Both types of optical fiber identifiers are essential tools for technicians to quickly and accurately identify fibers in optical networks.</p></p>
