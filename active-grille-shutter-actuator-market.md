Single Output actuators are generally simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for vehicles with basic grille shutter systems. On the other hand, Dual Output actuators are more complex and versatile, ideal for advanced grille shutter systems that require independent control of multiple sets of shutters for optimized aerodynamics and efficiency.</p></p>
