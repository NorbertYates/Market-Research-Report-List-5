<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switch</li><li>Layer 3 Switch</li><li>Layer 4 Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer switches are networking devices that operate at different levels of the OSI model. Layer 2 switches operate at the data link layer, providing Ethernet connectivity and switching based on MAC addresses. Layer 3 switches operate at the network layer, capable of routing IP packets. Layer 4 switches operate at the transport layer, allowing for advanced traffic management based on port numbers. Each type of switch caters to specific networking needs and offers varying levels of functionality and flexibility in managing network traffic.</p></p>
