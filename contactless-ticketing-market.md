<p><p>Contactless ticketing can be classified into three main market types: NFC, code scanning, and other. NFC technology allows users to simply tap their smartphone or card on a reader to access tickets. Code scanning involves scanning a QR code or barcode displayed on a mobile device to validate tickets. The "other" category includes alternative contactless technologies such as Bluetooth and RFID. These market types offer convenient and efficient ways for consumers to access tickets without the need for physical contact or paper tickets.</p></p>
