<p><p>Long-hole drilling rigs are used in the mining industry to create holes for blasting and ore extraction. There are three main types of long-hole drilling rigs: Tophammer rigs use a percussive drilling method at the top of the hole, Rotary rigs use a rotating drilling bit to bore the hole, and Down-the-Hole rigs use a pneumatic hammer at the bottom. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors such as rock type, hole depth, and desired drilling speed.</p></p>
