<p><p>Automotive starters are essential components of a vehicle's engine, responsible for initiating the engine's combustion process. There are three main types of automotive starters: Electric starters are the most common type, utilizing an electric motor to crank the engine. Pneumatic starters use compressed air to crank the engine, commonly found in heavy-duty vehicles. Hydraulic starters use hydraulic pressure to start the engine, often used in industrial applications. Each type of starter offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the vehicle and application.</p></p>
