<p><strong>The Single-Phase Stepper Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable-reluctance (VR)</li><li>Permanent Magnet (PM)</li><li>Hybrid (HB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-phase stepper motors are available in three main types: Variable-reluctance (VR), Permanent Magnet (PM), and Hybrid (HB). VR stepper motors have a simple design with a stator and rotor that relies on variations in magnetic reluctance to produce motion. PM stepper motors use permanent magnets on the rotor for more efficient and precise movement. Hybrid stepper motors combine the features of both VR and PM motors, offering improved performance and accuracy in a compact design.</p></p>
