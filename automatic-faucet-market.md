<p><p>Automatic faucets come in two main types: deck mounted and wall mounted faucets. Deck mounted faucets are installed directly onto the sink or countertop, providing a sleek and modern look. Wall mounted faucets, on the other hand, are attached to the wall above the sink, allowing for more counter space and easier cleaning. Both types offer touchless operation, making them more hygienic and convenient for users. The choice between deck mounted and wall mounted faucets ultimately depends on the specific design and layout of the space.</p></p>
