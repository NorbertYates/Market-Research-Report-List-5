<p><p>The Specialty Cosmetic Ingredients market can be categorized into different types based on their functions and properties. Emollients are ingredients that provide moisturizing and softening effects on the skin. Surfactants help in cleansing and foaming properties. Anionics are negatively charged surfactants, while non-anionics are neutral. Cationics have positively charged ions and are used for conditioning. Amphoterics can act as both anions and cations. Other specialty cosmetic ingredients include antioxidants, preservatives, and fragrances, among others. Each type plays a specific role in enhancing the effectiveness and quality of cosmetic products.</p></p>
