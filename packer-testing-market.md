<p><strong>The Packer Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Packer Hydraulic Conductivity Test</li><li>Hydraulic Conductivity Testing of Double Packers</li><li>Withdrawal Packer Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packer testing market consists of three main types: Single Packer Hydraulic Conductivity Test, Hydraulic Conductivity Testing of Double Packers, and Withdrawal Packer Testing. The Single Packer Hydraulic Conductivity Test involves measuring the hydraulic conductivity of a single packer in a borehole. Hydraulic Conductivity Testing of Double Packers involves measuring the hydraulic conductivity between two packers in a borehole. Withdrawal Packer Testing is used to measure the in-situ hydraulic conductivity of the formation during the withdrawal of the packer from the borehole.</p></p>
