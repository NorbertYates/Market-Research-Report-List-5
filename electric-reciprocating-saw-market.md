<p><p>The electric reciprocating saw market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired type saws require a constant connection to a power source, offering consistent power but limited mobility. Wireless type saws, on the other hand, operate on rechargeable batteries, providing more freedom of movement but potentially sacrificing power over time. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with wired saws preferred for heavy-duty applications and wireless saws ideal for portability and convenience.</p></p>
