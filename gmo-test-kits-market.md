<p><p>GMO test kits are used for detecting genetically modified organisms in food products. There are two main types of GMO test kits in the market - PCR and ELISA. PCR kits use polymerase chain reaction technology to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences of GMOs. ELISA kits use enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay to detect specific proteins or antibodies associated with GMOs. Both PCR and ELISA kits are commonly used in food testing laboratories to ensure compliance with GMO labeling regulations and to meet consumer demand for GMO-free products.</p></p>
