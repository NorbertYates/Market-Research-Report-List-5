<p><p>Classic trainers are basic indoor bike trainers that provide resistance through a magnetic or fluid mechanism. They are affordable and simple to use but do not offer advanced features like automatic resistance adjustments or connectivity to training apps. Smart trainers, on the other hand, are high-tech options that can automatically adjust resistance based on virtual courses or training programs. They also often have Bluetooth or ANT+ connectivity for syncing with apps and tracking performance data. Smart trainers are more expensive but offer a more interactive and immersive training experience.</p></p>
