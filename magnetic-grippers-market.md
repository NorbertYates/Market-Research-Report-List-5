<p><p>Magnetic grippers are classified into two main types: Electromagnetic Grippers and Permanent Magnet Grippers. Electromagnetic Grippers use an electric current to generate a magnetic field to hold onto objects, offering adjustable gripping force. Permanent Magnet Grippers, on the other hand, use permanent magnets to create a strong and constant magnetic field for gripping applications. Both types of magnetic grippers are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and robotics for handling and manipulating metallic objects.</p></p>
