<p><ul><li>Omnidirectional</li><li>Cardioid</li><li>Omni</li><li>Supercardioid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lavalier microphones come in different types, each suited for specific recording needs. Omnidirectional lavalier mics pick up sound from all directions, making them ideal for capturing ambient noise. Cardioid mics focus on sound in front and filter out background noise. Omni mics excel at capturing a wide range of sound sources. Supercardioid mics have a narrow pickup pattern, minimizing feedback and ambient noise. Each type offers unique benefits for different recording situations.</p></p>
