<p><p>Wall Mount Humidity Controllers come in two main types: Automatic and Toggle. Automatic controllers are designed to regulate humidity levels automatically, without the need for manual adjustments. They feature sensors that detect the current humidity levels and adjust accordingly. On the other hand, Toggle controllers require manual adjustments to set the desired humidity level. Users must physically toggle the controller to increase or decrease the humidity levels as needed. Both types offer convenience and control over maintaining optimal humidity levels in various spaces.</p></p>
