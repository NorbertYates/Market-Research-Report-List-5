<p><p>Industrial vacuum systems market is segmented into three main types of pumps: Ion Pumps, Titanium Sublimation Pumps, and Non-Evaporable Getter Pumps. Ion Pumps operate by generating a high-speed ion stream to create a vacuum, Titanium Sublimation Pumps utilize titanium as a material that sublimes to create a vacuum, and Non-Evaporable Getter Pumps use getter materials to absorb gases and maintain vacuum levels. These pumps are essential in various industrial processes that require precise control of vacuum levels.</p></p>
