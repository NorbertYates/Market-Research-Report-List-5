<p><p>Accumulators are used in hydraulic systems to store energy and smooth out pressure fluctuations. There are four main types of accumulators on the market: Weight-Loaded Piston Type, Diaphragm (Bladder) Type, Spring Type, and Hydro-Pneumatic Piston Type. Weight-Loaded Piston Type accumulators use the weight of a piston to store energy, while Diaphragm (Bladder) Type uses a flexible bladder to separate gas and fluid. Spring Type accumulators utilize a spring to store energy, and Hydro-Pneumatic Piston Type accumulators use a combination of gas and liquid to store energy.</p></p>
