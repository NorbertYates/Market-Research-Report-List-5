<p><p>Seed lasers are widely used in applications such as continuous wave (CW) lasers and pulsed fiber lasers. CW lasers operate with a continuous output beam, making them suitable for tasks such as cutting, welding, and telecommunications. Pulsed fiber lasers, on the other hand, generate high-power beams in short bursts, making them ideal for applications like material processing, medical procedures, and military uses. Seed lasers serve as the initial power source for both types of laser systems, providing the necessary energy to create and amplify laser pulses.</p></p>
