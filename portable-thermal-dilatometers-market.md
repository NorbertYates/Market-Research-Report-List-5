<p><p>Portable thermal dilatometers are used for measuring the dimensional changes in materials due to temperature variations. There are three main types of portable thermal dilatometers in the market: capacitance thermal dilatometers, connecting rod thermal dilatometers, and optical thermal dilatometers. Capacitance thermal dilatometers measure the change in capacitance due to thermal expansion, connecting rod thermal dilatometers use a connecting rod to measure displacement, and optical thermal dilatometers measure changes in length optically. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
