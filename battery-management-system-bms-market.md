<p><p>The Battery Management System (BMS) Market is divided into three main types: Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. </p><p>Distributed BMS involves individual control units for each battery module, providing more flexibility and redundancy. Centralized BMS uses a single control unit for the entire battery system, offering centralized monitoring and control. Modular BMS is a combination of the two, with multiple control units that can be integrated or separated as needed for scalability and customization. Each type has its own benefits and applications based on the specific requirements of the battery system.</p></p>
