<p><p>Copper brazed plate heat exchangers are available in two main types: multi-circuit heat exchangers and single-circuit heat exchangers. Multi-circuit heat exchangers are designed with multiple channels for fluid flow, allowing for efficient heat transfer between different mediums. Single-circuit heat exchangers, on the other hand, have a single fluid circuit, making them suitable for applications where only one medium needs to be heated or cooled. Both types offer excellent thermal performance and durability due to their copper brazed construction.</p></p>
