<p><p>Femoral venous catheters are used for various medical procedures, with two main types available in the market. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae are single-lumen catheters used for procedures like hemodialysis and hemodynamic monitoring. On the other hand, bipolar femoral arterial cannulae are dual-lumen catheters that allow for simultaneous infusion and monitoring. Both types are designed to provide efficient and safe access to the femoral artery, making them essential tools for healthcare professionals in critical care settings.</p></p>
