<p><ul><li>Low Pressure System</li><li>Medium Pressure System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV pool systems are an effective way to keep swimming pools clean and safe by using ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and contaminants. Low-pressure systems use lamps that emit UV-C light with a lower intensity, making them more affordable but requiring longer exposure times. Medium-pressure systems have lamps that emit UV-C light at a higher intensity, providing quicker and more thorough disinfection. Low-pressure systems are more common in residential pools, while medium-pressure systems are often used in commercial pools or larger residential pools.</p></p>
