<p><p>Bentonite mining market is categorized into three main types based on the composition of the mineral - Sodium, Calcium, and Others. Sodium bentonite is predominantly used in drilling fluids, foundry sands, and iron ore pelletizing due to its swelling properties. Calcium bentonite, on the other hand, is commonly used in animal feed and cat litter due to its absorbent properties. The Others category includes bentonite with unique compositions and applications, such as potassium bentonite used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
