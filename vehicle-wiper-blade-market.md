<p><p>Vehicle wiper blades are available in three main types: boneless wiper blades, bone wiper blades, and hybrid wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades have a sleek and flat design with no metal frame, providing a more aerodynamic and efficient wiping performance. Bone wiper blades have a traditional metal frame construction, offering durability and strength. Hybrid wiper blades combine elements of both boneless and bone wiper blades to provide a balance of performance and convenience. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of vehicle owners.</p></p>
