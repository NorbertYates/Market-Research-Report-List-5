<p><p>Vane Anemometer and Cup Anemometer are two types of High Temperature Anemometers used for measuring wind speed. Vane Anemometers have a rotating vane that aligns with the wind direction to measure velocity, while Cup Anemometers have rotating cups that catch the wind to measure speed. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as aviation, meteorology, and environmental monitoring. Vane Anemometers are preferred for measuring wind direction accurately, while Cup Anemometers are known for their durability and reliability in harsh weather conditions.</p></p>
