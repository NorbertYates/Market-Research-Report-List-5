<p><p>Electron beam welding machines are categorized into high vacuum beam welding machines and medium vacuum beam welding machines based on the level of vacuum environment in which the welding process takes place. High vacuum beam welding machines provide a more controlled and cleaner welding environment, making them suitable for precision applications where cleanliness and minimal contamination are critical. On the other hand, medium vacuum beam welding machines offer a balance between control and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for a wide range of welding applications.</p></p>
