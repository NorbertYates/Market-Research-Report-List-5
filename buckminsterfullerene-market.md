<p><p>The Buckminsterfullerene market is categorized into various types based on the structure of the carbon molecules. Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNT) are long, cylindrical tubes made of a single layer of carbon atoms. Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNT) consist of multiple layers of carbon atoms arranged in a tube. Bucky Balls are spherical carbon molecules. Nano Rods are elongated carbon structures. Each type of Buckminsterfullerene has unique properties and applications, creating a diverse market for carbon nanomaterials.</p></p>
