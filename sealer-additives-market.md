<p><p>Sealer additives are substances added to sealers to enhance their performance. There are three main types of sealer additives: powder, gelatinous, and others. Powder additives are finely ground substances that are easily mixed into sealer solutions. Gelatinous additives are thicker, jelly-like substances that provide a more viscous texture to the sealer. Other types of additives include liquid and granular forms that offer different benefits such as improved adhesion, quicker drying times, or enhanced durability. Each type of additive serves a specific purpose in enhancing the effectiveness of sealers.</p></p>
