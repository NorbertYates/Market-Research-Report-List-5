<p><p>X-ray Diffraction Instrument Market can be divided into two main types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is used for analyzing powdered samples, providing information on the crystalline structure of materials such as pharmaceuticals, minerals, and polymers. Single-crystal XRD, on the other hand, is used to analyze single crystals and determine their atomic and molecular structures. This type is commonly used in the study of small organic molecules, inorganic compounds, and proteins. Both types of XRD instruments play a crucial role in materials science, chemistry, and biology research.</p></p>
