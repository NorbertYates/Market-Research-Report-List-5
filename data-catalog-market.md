<p><p>Data catalog markets can be categorized into two main types: Cloud and On-Premises. Cloud data catalogs are hosted and accessed through a cloud-based platform, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access. On the other hand, On-Premises data catalogs are deployed and maintained within an organization's own infrastructure, providing greater control and security over data. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with Cloud data catalogs being ideal for organizations looking for agility and cost-efficiency, while On-Premises data catalogs are preferred by organizations with strict data governance and compliance requirements.</p></p>
