<p><p>Loan origination tools are classified into two main market types: On-demand (Cloud) and On-premise. On-demand, or cloud-based, loan origination tools are accessed and managed online through a web browser, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for on-site infrastructure. On the other hand, on-premise loan origination tools are installed and maintained locally within an organization's own servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both market types cater to different preferences and requirements of financial institutions.</p></p>
