<p><p>VOC recovery and abatement technology market includes various types of solutions for controlling emissions of volatile organic compounds in industrial processes. Oxidizers, filtration systems, membrane separations, scrubbers and strippers, condensation, and adsorbent systems are all different methods utilized for VOC control. Oxidizers use heat or chemicals to break down VOCs, filtration systems use physical barriers to capture VOCs, and membrane separations utilize permeable membranes. Scrubbers and strippers use liquid solutions to remove VOCs, condensation cools and condenses VOCs, and adsorbent systems trap VOCs on surfaces.</p></p>
