<p><p>A Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillator (WCD) is a small device worn on the body that monitors the heart's rhythm and delivers a shock to restore normal heart rhythm in case of a life-threatening arrhythmia. WCDs are typically prescribed to patients at high risk of sudden cardiac arrest, such as those recovering from a heart attack or those awaiting placement of an implantable cardioverter defibrillator (ICD).</p><p>The Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators Market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 4.3%.
