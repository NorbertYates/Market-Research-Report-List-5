<p><p>Zebra Guidewire offers two main market types: Straight Guidewires and Elbow Guidewires. Straight Guidewires are designed for navigating through straight or slightly curved blood vessels, offering smooth insertion and easy maneuverability. Elbow Guidewires, on the other hand, are specifically designed for accessing vessels with challenging anatomical bends or angles. They provide greater control and flexibility when navigating through complex pathways. Both types of guidewires are essential tools in minimally invasive procedures, helping physicians guide catheters and other medical devices to the desired location within the body.</p></p>
