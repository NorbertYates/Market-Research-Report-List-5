<p><p>Tubular ceramic membrane technology is classified into two main types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration membranes are used for larger particle separation, while ultrafiltration membranes are used for smaller particle separation. Microfiltration membranes are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment. Ultrafiltration membranes are typically used in industries where higher purity and smaller particle separation are required, such as protein purification and water treatment. Both types offer efficient separation and have applications in various industries.</p></p>
