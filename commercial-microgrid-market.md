<p><p>Commercial Microgrid Market includes three main types: AC Microgrid System, DC Microgrid System, and Hybrid Microgrid System. AC Microgrid System operates on alternating current, suitable for applications connected to the utility grid. DC Microgrid System operates on direct current, ideal for remote locations or areas with limited access to the grid. Hybrid Microgrid System combines AC and DC components, providing flexibility and reliability. Each system has its unique advantages and is designed to meet specific energy needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
