<p><p>Shaft collars are devices used to secure components onto shafts. There are two main types of shaft collars in the market - clamping shaft collars and set screw shaft collars. Clamping shaft collars feature a split design with a screw that clamps down on the shaft for a secure hold. On the other hand, set screw shaft collars have a single screw that tightens against the shaft to prevent slippage. Both types offer reliable and cost-effective solutions for securing components onto shafts in various applications.</p></p>
