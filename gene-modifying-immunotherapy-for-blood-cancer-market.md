<p><p>Gene Modifying Immunotherapy for Blood Cancer involves modifying a patient's immune cells to better target and attack cancerous cells. CAR T-cell Therapy involves genetically engineering a patient's T cells to express chimeric antigen receptors, which can recognize and destroy cancer cells. Both therapies have shown promising results in treating blood cancers such as leukemia and lymphoma.</p></p>
