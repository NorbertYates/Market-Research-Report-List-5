<p><p>The organic filler market can be classified into two main types based on the type of polymer they are used with: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset fillers are used with polymers that undergo a chemical reaction to harden and cannot be reshaped once set, while thermoplastic fillers are used with polymers that can be heated and reshaped multiple times without undergoing a chemical change. Both types of fillers play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and properties of polymer materials in various industries.</p></p>
